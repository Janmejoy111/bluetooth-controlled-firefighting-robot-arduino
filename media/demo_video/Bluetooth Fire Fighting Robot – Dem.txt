Bluetooth Fire Fighting Robot – Demo Video

This video demonstrates the working of the Bluetooth Controlled Fire Fighting Robot built using Arduino Nano. The robot can be controlled wirelessly through a smartphone using an HC-05 Bluetooth module.

The system uses three flame sensors to detect fire. When a flame is detected, the robot stops automatically, activates an alarm using a buzzer and LED, and sprays water using a relay-controlled 5V water pump. A servo motor is used to aim the nozzle toward the fire source.
