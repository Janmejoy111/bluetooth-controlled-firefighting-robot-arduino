Bluetooth Fire Fighting Robot – Component Requirements

Microcontroller

* Arduino Nano (ATmega328P)

Communication Module

* HC-05 Bluetooth Module

Motor Control

* L298N Motor Driver Module
* 2 × BO DC Motors
* Robot Wheels

Fire Detection System

* 3 × Flame Sensor Modules

Fire Suppression System

* 5V Mini Water Pump
* Water Pipe / Nozzle
* Small Water Container

Actuators

* SG90 Servo Motor (for aiming the water nozzle)

Control and Switching

* 1 × 5V Relay Module
* 1 × 2-Pin Power Switch

Alert System

* 1 × LED Indicator
* 1 × Buzzer / Mini Speaker

Power System

* 18650 Li-ion Batteries
* DC-DC Buck Converter (LM2596 or similar)

Mechanical Structure

* Wooden base chassis
* Two-layer (two-storey) platform structure
* 4 × Support bolts (for vertical frame)
* 12 × Nuts and bolts for mounting components

Miscellaneous

* Breadboard / PCB
* Jumper Wires (Male-Male / Male-Female)
* Connecting Wires
* Zip ties / mounting clips
